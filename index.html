About Matcon Productions

Doing simple things well

Matcon Productions Limited is a founder-led parent company for multiple creative and entrepreneurial ventures. We operate across three main service areas - Education, Media and Property - blending the knowledge of each sector to provide innovative solutions that actually work.

Education

Enhancing the learning process and achieving measurable outcomes with radical curriculum design, engaging digital platforms and personalised tutoring support.

Media

Transforming ideas into sustainable brands and defining their vision with professional copywriting, digital content creation and graphic design.

Property

Preserving assets and increasing their value with maintenance-led property management for both residential and commercial dwellings.
